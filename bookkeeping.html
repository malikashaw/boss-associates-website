FAQ

Questions about bookkeeping

Straight answers to the questions we hear most.

How often will my books be updated?

Most clients are on a monthly cadence, with reconciliations and a summary each period. If your business moves faster, we can talk about a schedule that fits.

I'm months behind — can you help?

Absolutely. Catch-up bookkeeping is one of the most common things we do. We'll bring your records current and then keep them that way.

Do you offer training if I want to learn myself?

Yes — that's a big part of what we do. Check out our Bookkeeping Training to learn the skill, or combine training with done-for-you bookkeeping.
