BOSS CERTIFICATE VERIFICATION — BRANDING UPDATE

1. Open your “Boss Certificate Verification” Google Apps Script project.
2. Click Index.html.
3. Find the current <style> block near the top.
4. Replace only that <style>...</style> block with the contents of BRANDED_STYLE_BLOCK.txt.
5. Do not delete or replace your form fields, JavaScript, or google.script.run code.
6. Click Save.
7. Click Deploy > Manage deployments.
8. Click the pencil/edit icon on the current Web App deployment.
9. Under Version, choose “New version,” then click Deploy.
10. Keep the same /exec URL. The website is already connected to it.

IMPORTANT FOR EMBEDDING
In Code.gs, your doGet() should allow the page to appear inside your website iframe. It should end like this:

function doGet() {
  return HtmlService.createHtmlOutputFromFile('Index')
    .setTitle('BOSS Certificate Verification')
    .setXFrameOptionsMode(HtmlService.XFrameOptionsMode.ALLOWALL);
}

If your doGet() already contains other settings or template code, keep those and add only:
.setXFrameOptionsMode(HtmlService.XFrameOptionsMode.ALLOWALL)
