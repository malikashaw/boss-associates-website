Verification Help

Everything you need to confirm a certificate

How do I verify a certificate?

Enter the certificate ID or the details requested by the tool above. It will confirm whether the certificate was issued by BOSS Associates LLC and to whom.

I'm an employer — what does verification confirm?

A successful result confirms that the named individual completed the corresponding BOSS Associates course and was issued a valid certificate of completion.

The tool isn't showing a result. What now?

Double-check the certificate ID for typos. If it still doesn't verify, please contact us with the name and approximate completion date and we'll look into it.
