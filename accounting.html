FAQ

Questions about accounting

Straight answers to the questions we hear most.

What's the difference between bookkeeping and accounting?

Bookkeeping records the day-to-day transactions; accounting turns those records into reconciled statements and insight. Many clients need both, and we handle them together so nothing is duplicated or missed.

Can you get my books caught up first?

Yes. If you're behind, we can bring your records current and reconciled before setting up an ongoing rhythm, so everything moving forward is accurate.

Will I understand the reports?

That's the point. We don't just hand over statements, we walk you through them so you know exactly what each number is telling you.
